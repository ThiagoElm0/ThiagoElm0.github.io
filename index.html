Warren travel plan

This map displays the complete route, with each marked point representing a location where supernatural manifestations have occurred or are known to have occurred. Additionally, all of these locations have been visited by the Warren couple, as they are the main focus of my travel plan.

The plan is to spend 15 days during this journey, and below you will find the itinerary that includes the hotels, restaurants, and other places I intend to visit.

Pittston

I plan to stay in the city of Pittston for 3 days, during which I will use the time to visit the former home of the Smurl family, Frances Slocum State Park, and other attractions.

During my trip, I plan to indulge in some delicious fast food options, as they are easily accessible and known for their tasty offerings.

Hotel: Best Western Plus Wilkes Barre-Scranton Airport Hotel

Monroe

In Monroe, I plan to stay for 4 days to study at the New England Society for Psychic Research. I want to learn about paranormal activities, demonic possession, and unexplained phenomena. During my visit, I'll attend workshops, lectures, and explore their extensive library. Interacting with experts and sharing experiences will help broaden my understanding of the occult.

I will dine at a nearby Italian restaurant called Flames Restaurant, located close to the research society.

Southington

In Southington, I will spend 3 days exploring the haunting Snedeker Family House, which was infested with paranormal activities. Additionally, I plan to visit parks like Southington Memorial Park and dine at local restaurants, among other activities.

It will be a chance to immerse myself in the world of the unknown and experience unique encounters related to the supernatural.

Burrillville

The last city I will visit is Burrillville, where the events portrayed in The Conjuring take place. Not only that, but the local cemetery also has the tomb of the alleged witch responsible for the curse of the house. I will spend 5 days in this city, 3 of them camping at the haunted house and 2 in the town.

My meals will be at The Taco Shop restaurant, and I will be staying at Grace Note Farm for accommodation.
